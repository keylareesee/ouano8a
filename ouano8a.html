Climate Change Facts

Climate Change Impacts

  • Global temperatures have risen by 1.2°C since the 19th century.
  • Sea levels are rising at an accelerating rate, endangering coastal communities.
  • Extreme weather events like hurricanes and wildfires are becoming more frequent and intense.

Environmental Challenges

  • Over 1 million species face extinction due to climate change and habitat loss.
  • Deforestation contributes to 10% of global warming emissions.
  • Ocean acidification from increased CO₂ threatens marine life and coral reefs.

How to Fact Check

To fact-check environmental information:

  • Use trusted sources like NASA, NOAA, and the IPCC.
  • Cross-check facts across multiple reputable websites.
  • Be cautious of sources with clear biases.
